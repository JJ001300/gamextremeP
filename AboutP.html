Our History

Since its inception in the year 2000, Xtremeway Corporation, a retail, wholesale, and e-commerce distribution company, has been committed to providing top-of-the-line gaming, entertainment, and tech needs. With more than 20 years in the industry, we have already successfully opened over 20 stores across the Philippines through our trade names The Vault, WhiteHaus, and GameXtreme. By 2022, Xtremeway Corporationaims to open at least 20 more stores across the Philippines.

The past years have proven Xtremeway Corporation to be a trusted retailer for gaming and mobile needs. Now, Xtremeway Corporation aims to partner with more global brands to make quality products more accessible to the Philippine market.
